# RevenueOS AI vision

RevenueOS AI will be the trusted AI teammate for professionals whose success depends on durable human relationships.

**Mission:** Eliminate administrative work from relationship-driven professions by building AI teammates that remember, reason and act.

**Core principle:** Automate work, not relationships.

Important context is routinely lost across conversations, notes and disconnected systems. RevenueOS will help a person preserve that context, understand what matters and prepare the next best action. It will sit alongside CRMs, ATSs, email, calendars and communication platforms rather than replace their role as systems of record.

Sales Brain is the first product. Recruitment Brain and Customer Success Brain will reuse the same organisation, interaction, memory, workflow, audit and integration foundations while keeping product-specific language and decisions separate.

The product is human-led, evidence-backed and consent-aware. It never captures a conversation implicitly, treats model output as reviewable rather than truth, and requires approval before consequential external actions.
